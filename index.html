Our Journey

Hreenkar Creation and Kambeshwar Agencies represent the entrepreneurial spirit of two dedicated brothers who share a vision of bringing premium quality apparel and innerwear brands to every corner of Goa. What started as a passion for quality and customer satisfaction has evolved into one of Goa's most trusted distribution networks.

Operating from our strategically located facility at Cosmos Center in Mapusa, we have established ourselves as authorized distributors for India's most prestigious brands. Our commitment extends beyond mere distribution - we believe in building lasting partnerships with retailers and ensuring that consumers across Goa have access to authentic, premium quality products.

With our dual entity structure - Hreenkar Creation (GST: 30AACPB7378Q1ZH) and Kambeshwar Agencies (GST: 30AOEPB9968G1ZZ) - we offer comprehensive distribution services that cater to diverse business needs across the state.
