About Hreenkar Creation

Leading Premium Brand Distributor in Goa

Who We Are

Hreenkar Creation is the leading authorized distributor of premium innerwear and apparel brands across Goa. Operating from our strategically located facility at COSMOS CENTER in Mapusa, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for retailers and wholesalers throughout the state.

What We Offer

As an authorized distributor, we provide 100% authentic, brand-approved products with GST certification. We serve 600+ retail partners across North and South Goa with competitive wholesale rates, flexible payment terms, and comprehensive business support.
